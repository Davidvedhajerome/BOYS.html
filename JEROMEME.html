1.BINGO GAME

Bingo is a game of chance played with randomly drawn numbers which players match against numbers that have been pre-printed on 5×5 cards. The game is named after its best-known variation, Bingo, in which players mark off numbers on a card as they are announced by a caller, with the winner being the first person to mark off all their numbers. There are many variations of the game, with the most common being 75-ball and 90-ball bingo.Bingo is a popular game for all ages and can be played in both large and small groups. It is a great way to socialize and have fun with friends and family. Bingo is easy to learn and can be played for both fun and prizes.
